About US

Who We Are

Enivion Tech is a trusted renewable energy partner with 13+ years of experience in delivering reliable wind, solar, hydro, battery storage, EV charging, and railway electrification projects. With a proven portfolio that includes Kerala’s first IPP wind project, 50+ MW of solar installations, 300+ EV charging stations, and 3,000+ km of railway electrification, we bring technical excellence and on-time execution across diverse energy domains. Our commitment is to maximize energy yield, minimize downtime, and support India’s sustainable energy transition.

We specialize in end-to-end project delivery—covering feasibility studies, engineering design, procurement, construction, commissioning, and O&M. Our expertise spans smart turbine solutions, award-winning solar EPC, advanced lithium-ion storage with BMS, automated hydro turbine systems, and 137/25 kV traction substations. By combining innovation with field-proven systems, we enable utilities, businesses, and communities to reduce costs, improve reliability, and achieve measurable decarbonization.

Our Mission

Our mission is to accelerate India’s transition to clean, reliable, and cost-effective renewable energy. We achieve this by delivering high-impact projects in solar, wind, storage, EV charging, hydro, and railway electrification—backed by award-winning execution and proven results. From Kerala’s first IPP wind project to 50+ MW of solar and nationwide charging networks, we combine technical excellence with sustainability goals. Every project we undertake is built on transparency, safety, and performance, ensuring long-term value for our partners and communities.

Over the past decade, Enivion Energy has consistently delivered projects that set industry benchmarks—whether it’s pioneering Kerala’s first IPP wind project, achieving 95%+ availability in wind operations, winning Kerala Government Solar Awards in 2018 and 2019, or deploying over 300 EV charging stations nationwide. With expertise across six key energy domains, we bridge innovation and reliability, helping India move closer to a sustainable, low-carbon future.
