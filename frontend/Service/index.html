Our Services

What We Offer

Full‑stack services spanning design, build, and operations—engineered to deliver reliable performance, predictable cost, and safe operation throughout the asset lifetime.

Design & Engineering

Feasibility-driven designs tailored to site and grid requirements.

  • Wind and solar resource assessment
  • Civil, structural, and geotechnical design
  • Turbine/solar layout optimization
  • Electrical balance of plant studies
  • Energy modeling and yield analysis

EPC & Commissioning

Turnkey project delivery with disciplined execution.

  • Procurement and logistics management
  • Civil, mechanical, and electrical installation
  • Substation integration and grid interconnection
  • Performance validation and testing

Energy Storage Integration

Battery systems that enhance reliability and grid stability.

  • Lithium-ion battery storage systems (BESS)
  • Advanced Battery Management Systems (BMS)
  • Real-time monitoring and control
  • Peak shaving, backup power, renewable smoothing

Operations & Maintenance

Proven track record of 95%+ asset availability.

  • 24/7 monitoring and fault diagnosis
  • Predictive and preventive maintenance
  • Spare parts and warranty coordination
  • Condition-based turbine and inverter servicing

Grid Services & Compliance

Seamless grid integration and utility coordination.

  • Utility liaison and approvals
  • Grid interconnection studies
  • Relay coordination and protection settings
  • Reactive power and frequency regulation

Asset Performance Analytics

Data-driven tools to maximize energy yield.

  • SCADA-based performance monitoring
  • Anomaly and degradation detection
  • Predictive diagnostics and alerts
  • Portfolio benchmarking and insights

Safety & Compliance

Zero-accident execution through global standards.

  • Safety-first protocols and quality audits
  • International code compliance (IEC, IS standards)
  • Environmental flow and impact management
  • Lock-out/tag-out and safe work procedures

Support & Training

Building strong operational capability for partners.

  • Operator and staff onboarding programs
  • SOPs and emergency response training
  • Preventive maintenance playbooks
  • Remote and on-site technical support
